Pk audio is company designing high end studio microhones with focus on great technical parameters with preserving sound flavour of original vintage microphones. All microphones are handmade with great precission measured and tested on professional laboratory equipment.

M49


Large membrane Condenser tube microphone based on legendary type U47. Electrical circuit is redesigned and allows use of different tubes which can give microhone different result. Mostly is PK47 used for vocals as in past was used in a most of best sounding records in the world. Microhone is favourite for known proximity efekt which makes vocals big but still smoothly rounded as it fits for the vast majority of female and male voices. We uses M7 capsules which were used en early models of U47. More info about microphone PK47 here.(Odkaz na další stránk kde se otevřou podrobnosti o mikrfofonu, další fotky atd,..)
